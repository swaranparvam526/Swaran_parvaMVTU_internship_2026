WELCOME TO CAMBRIDGE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

Welcome to Cambridge Institute of Technology. The institution has emerged as a benchmark for holistic learning and institutional integrity. Set on a verdant 16-acre campus overlooking Krishnarajapuram and just 200 meters from Old Madras Road, the college offers exceptional connectivity across Bengaluru while providing a serene, future-ready environment for academic and personal growth.

About Cambridge Institute of Technology (CIT)

Year of Establishment: 2007

History

The Cambridge Institute of Technology (CIT), established in 2007, is a private engineering institution affiliated with VTU, Belagavi, and approved by AICTE. The institute follows a student-centric approach, integrating digital learning, hands-on projects, and industry-focused skills.

Founder's Message

The founder of CIT, Mr. Nithin, believes in the transformative power of education. His vision is to build an ecosystem that integrates technology with human values, preparing socially responsible professionals and innovators for a better world.
